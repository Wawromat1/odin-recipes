Popcornchicken (Mealprep)

Description

Crispy, bite-sized popcorn chicken served over fluffy rice akes the perfect balanced and satisfying meal prep option. The chicken is seasoned and golden on the outside, tender and juicy on the inside, pairing perfectly with simple steamed or seasoned rice. Add your favorite veggies or a light sauce for extra flavor.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast (boneless, skinless)
  • All-purpose flour
  • Cornstarch
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Paprika
  • Onion powder
  • Chili powder (optional)
  • Breadcrumbs or panko (optional, for extra crispiness)
  • Cooking oil (for frying or baking spray for oven version)

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Cook the rice with water and a pinch of salt according to package instructions.
  3. Let the rice steam for 5 minutes, then fluff with a fork.
  4. Cut the chicken breast into small, bite-sized pieces.
  5. Season the chicken with salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and onion powder.
  6. Prepare three bowls: one with flour and cornstarch, one with whisked eggs and milk, and one with breadcrumbs (optional).
  7. Coat each chicken piece in the flour mixture, then dip into the egg mixture, and finally coat with breadcrumbs.
  8. Heat oil in a pan or preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  9. Fry the chicken until golden brown and fully cooked (about 4–6 minutes), or bake for 18–20 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  10. Prepare optional vegetables by steaming or sautéing them.
  11. Let everything cool slightly.
  12. Divide rice, popcorn chicken, and vegetables evenly into meal prep containers.
  13. Store in the refrigerator for up to 4 days.
