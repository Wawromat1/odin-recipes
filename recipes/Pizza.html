Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with a round, flat base of baked dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of ingredients such as vegetables, meats, and herbs. It originated in Italy, especially in the city of Naples, and has become one of the most loved foods around the world. Pizza can be simple, like a classic Margherita with tomato, mozzarella, and basil, or creative with many different toppings. It is usually baked in an oven and served hot, making it a favorite meal for people of all ages.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough.
  3. Spread tomato sauce on the dough.
  4. Add mozzarella cheese.
  5. Add basil and drizzle olive oil.
  6. Bake until golden and the cheese is melted.
  7. Slice and serve.
